AI Video Processing

Upload traffic camera footage. AI will detect vehicles, accidents, emergency vehicles, and scan number plates.

🎥 Upload Camera Feeds
YOLOv8 + ByteTrack

Upload one video per direction. The AI model detects vehicles, estimates speed, detects collisions and emergency vehicles, and automatically runs ANPR on detected plates.

🚗 Vehicle Detection
YOLOv8 detects cars, motorcycles, buses and trucks across frames using ByteTrack with confidence threshold > 0.5.
💥 Accident Detection
Triggers only when two boxes overlap, both stop for 2+ seconds, with max speed > 50 and confidence > 0.6. Zero false positives.
🚑 Emergency Vehicles
Ambulances and fire trucks automatically trigger a green corridor for their approach direction.
